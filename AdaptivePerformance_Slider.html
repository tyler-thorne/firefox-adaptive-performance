About Adaptive Performance

Firefox's Adaptive Performance gives you control over how the browser balances speed, battery life, and resource usage based on what matters most to you.

Performance mode maximizes speed by allocating more system resources—ideal for multiple tabs, heavy web applications, or media-rich content. Firefox responds faster, but uses more CPU and battery.

Power Saving mode reduces energy consumption by up to 15% through optimized background processes and intelligent resource management. This extends battery life on laptops and mobile devices. Firefox serves hundreds of millions of users, and these small efficiencies add up to meaningful global environmental impact.

Balanced mode is Firefox's intelligent default, automatically tuned to provide excellent performance while managing resources efficiently. This mode adapts to your device and browsing patterns.
